Program Schedule

Here is a weekly schedule of the program. Walk on 6 days; take one day off. Use dumbbells, or other weights, at home or at the gym.

Days 1 to 6 Walk for at least 40 minutes at a brisk pace or one that makes you breathe heavy, but does not make you breathless. Split the session up if it suits you, but try to keep up the intensity. Day 2: Choose 8 dumbbell exercises from the dumbbell program, and do 3 sets of 12 exercise repetitions. If doing 8 exercises at once is too much, break it up into 4 exercises for two separate sessions. Day 3: Do the circuit training program. Day 4: Walk for 40 minutes. Day 5: Repeat the dumbbell program performed on day 2. Day 6: Repeat the dumbbell program performed on day 2 again. Day 7 Walk for 40 minutes, or take a rest day.
